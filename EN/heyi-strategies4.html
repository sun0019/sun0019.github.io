M&A and Restructuring

We have a cross-disciplinary team to serve clients and provide advisory services with regard to mergers, takeovers, strategic cooperation, asset restructuring and reorganization, and identifies and formulates strategic partnership to create the business opportunity.

  • Formulation and execution of listed company tender offer and takeover strategies
  • Shareholder dispute resolution for listed companies
  • Advice on corporate governance structure and strategies of maintaining independent management of listed companies
  • Public opinion monitoring and crisis management strategies for listed companies
  • Due diligence on corporate or asset restructuring and IPO, and formulation and analysis of restructuring plan

IPO Services

We advise clients on planning and structuring of an IPO or privatization from Chinese and international stock exchanges, provide assistance to clients on business, legal, regulatory, financial, tax, compliance aspects of an IPO or privatization, and helps clients in deal management.

  • Advice on IPO market options, one-stop services of restructuring assets and corporate entities
  • Pre-IPO review and advice on how to improve financial, tax, legal and operational compliance to meet listing requirements of a proposed stock exchange
  • Formulation and implementation of share split reform plans
  • Formation of stock incentive plans
  • Do’s and Don’ts for related party transaction and information disclosure
  • Training for directors and officers
  • Other related services (including deal management, underwriter referral, and roadshow)

Financial Advisory

We provide services on financing, acquisition and disposal of assets and shell companies.

  • Identify and Introduce investors for M&A and financing deals
  • Identify and Introduce targets for acquisition or disposal of assets/ shell companies
  • Evaluate investor candidates or potential targets and perform feasibility analysis
  • Conduct due diligence investigation on financing or asset acquisition/disposal
  • Assist client in negotiating transactions with potential investors
  • Delivery and closing support

Management Advisory

We provide one-stop services on legal, financial, tax, labor relations and other aspects that are crucial to the daily operation of enterprises to improve their compliance as well as operational efficiency.

  • Update clients on industry developments, changes in regulatory framework and legislations as well as interpret new industry policies
  • Provide advisory service on dispute resolution during client’s daily operation
  • Assist clients in their operational management and business negotiations, draft and review legal documents
  • Release information on asset disposal, strategic investment and financing through HEYI’s investment and financing information system to match buy side and sell side
  • Provide training to directors and officers on best practice for corporation compliance and management of risks associated with their fiduciary duties

Corporate Reorganization

We provide overall reorganization or restructuring solutions to companies in liquidity crisis.

  • Identification the value of clients’ core assets based on industrial, business and financial analyses
  • Formulation of debt or asset restructuring and/or bankruptcy reorganization solutions
  • Advice on and assistance in implementation of litigation and asset preservation strategies
  • Interim liquidity solutions and identification and introduction of strategic investors

NPL/NPA Disposal

Our advisory services cover due diligence investigation on, valuation of, and disposal of non-performing assets (NPAs) and assistance in obtaining financing needed for the parties involved in the transaction. Our assessment and service proposal takes into account of various factors including their intrinsic value, market situation, viable means of disposal of NPAs and identification of investors. Our mission is to maximize the value of clients’ NPAs.

Dispute Resolution

We provide clients with practical solutions to disputes arising from commercial transactions and coordinates legal team and financial institutions to advise client on structure transaction and terms. In addition, we assist clients in negotiating and consulting with relevant parties to recover their losses.

Tax Advisory

We provide tax services to state-owned enterprises, multinational corporations, listed companies, and start-ups. Our services include perennial tax advisory services, tax audits, tax planning, strategies for anti-avoidance investigations, tax authority communication and document preparation for related party transactions, non-resident corporate tax services, and tax risk management.
