About HEYI

HEYI at a Glance

Established in 2015, HEYI Group has offices in Beijing, Shanghai, the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macau Greater Bay Area and South Korea. Leveraging our diversified expertise and extensive experience in finance, law and taxation, we are committed to provide customized and innovative business solutions to clients on global scale. Our one-stop service and synergies with our global strategic partners are able to improve execution efficiency and satisfy client expectation.

Our team consists of professionals with diversified background, including bankers, lawyers, certified public accountants, tax advisors and entrepreneurs. With in-depth understanding of industry sectors, keen business insight and extensive experience, we interpret macro policies and analyze market environment to help clients identify and seize market opportunities and assist them to achieve milestones at each phase of their corporate life cycle.

Our Services

HEYI Group is committed to the improvement of clients' core competencies, with which they are able to adapt to the changes in economic, commercial and regulatory environments, and achieve business growth or transformation. Our service scope covers Advisory Services, Think Tank and Public Interests.

Think Tank

We perform academic and practical research on macroeconomic environment, industry dynamics, regulatory policies, insightful cases and innovative business solutions for clients, and provide big data analysis services to support decision-making in strategy formulation, business planning and operational management.

Public Interests

We are dedicated to public interest initiatives in joint efforts with other organizations, government institutions and social groups to eliminate poverty and provide medical aid to those who are in need, especially the orphans and children of migrant workers in poverty-stricken areas of China. We aim to improve their standard of living, education condition and wellbeing with donations and other types of sponsorship.

We believe that corporations should fulfill their social responsibilities and return earnings and resources to their communities. We welcome everyone to join our public interest initiatives to make the world a better place.

Strategic Cooperation

HEYI Group is a strategic partner of King & Wood Mallesons, and has strategic partnership with Ping An Trust, CDH Fund, Sinochem Asset Management Co., Ltd. and Huarong Yufu Capital. Our cooperation with these companies creates synergies in industrial expertise, business opportunities and client base.
